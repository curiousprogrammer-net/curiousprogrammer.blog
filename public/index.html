Moving to Cryogen (with AsciiDoc)

The Wordpress problem

I wanted to migrate away from the existing Wordpress-based hosting for my curiosprogrammer.net blog for a long time yet I have not found time to do that until now. Today, I’m finally moving towards much simpler approach using Cryogen as the underlying blog engine.

Cryogen ===

It’s a simple blog engine written in Clojure. It’s used by a number of people in Clojure community, including:

  • Sean Corfield: https://github.com/seancorfield/seancorfield.github.io

  • Jakub Holy: https://blog.jakubholy.net/2019/migrating-from-gatsby-to-cryogen/

I think it will well serve my needs and I hope that the friction for publishing posts (and the frustrations caused by Wordpress editor) is going to disappear.

AsciiDoc

I’ve found a couple of AsciiDoc-related links in Jakub Holy’s article and decided to give it a try. The promise is that it’s better than Markdown (lack of standard and extensibility).

See:
