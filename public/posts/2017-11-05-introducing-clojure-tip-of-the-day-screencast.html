Introducing Clojure Tip of the Day Screencast

I’ve started a new Clojure screencast Clojure Tip of the Day. It’s going to be a collection of short (~5 min) videos covering various Clojure tips, tools, and techniques. I’d like to add a new episode at least once a week.

The first episode is about Cider debugger. It was inspired by a comment on my Clojure Development Workflow with Spacemacs and Cider video tutorial.

You can find the episode on YouTube: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jHCch3-Yuac.

See Cider Debugging documentation for more details.
